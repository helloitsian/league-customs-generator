How it Works

This team sorter prioritizes role selection over rank, then does it's best to balance the teams by rank. It supports primary/secondary roles, fill, and auto-fill. To specify a player for fill, set their primary role as Fill and select any role for secondary (secondary role won't be used). Currently, secondary fill is not supported.
